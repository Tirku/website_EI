Corporate Profile

Eagleye InfoTech Company Limited (EI) is professionally managed and process driven company incorporated in Thailand in 2004. Setup to provide state of the art IT enabled solutions for the Steel and Metallurgical industries in Thailand and abroad, EI has since diversified into entire spectrum of Industries including Construction, logistics, Port etc. EI is committed to providing high quality IT solutions. Its range of services includes ERP Implementation, Off-shore Software Development, Internet & E-commerce, Y2K & EURO consultancy, IT Project Management and providing industry specific IT solutions.
 


 

EI's professional experience is backed by vast IT and industry experience of its technical experts and promoter. EI leverages on its executives' experience in implementing major projects worldwide. Its current product portfolio includes an integrated Materials Management System and Plant Maintenance System for the process & discreet industries. In addition to delivering highly customized client-server solutions and Web & E-Commerce applications, EI also has expertise in providing Oracle Applications implementation.

EI's Corporate Office in Bangkok, Thailand, has state-of-the-art hardware and software environment to permit development of applications targeted for the 2 tier (Client/ Server) and 3 tier architectures. This center has been set up purely as an IT solution provider unit.

EI's dedicated team of software professional and business managers with wide range of experience is backed up by vast industry experience available from Thailand and neighboring countries. This combination makes it possible to provide solutions to its client that is industry standard and is enriched with proven technologies.

In this continuously changing technological and business environment, EI is constantly updating itself by and acquiring new skills and capabilities to remain at the forefront of technology. To sustain the impressive growth it has shown in previous years, EI is constantly exploring new areas and forging partnerships worldwide with leaders in the IT industry.
