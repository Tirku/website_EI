Computerized Materials Management System (CMMS)
MMS is an enterprise-wide solution to resolve the material demand and fulfillment process of an organization. The system provides to identify, select and acquire needed materials and services economically, with high standards of quality and services in consideration to the business policies of the organization. MMS maintains an organization's purchasing and inventory management in a timely & organized manner that provides for essential accountability of the financial budget.

MMS is unique to handle not only the spare parts and consumables but also efficiently manage the raw material procurement, warehousing logistic and appropriate consumption. The raw material handling is a dedicated module in MMS which is dovetailed with all related business activities to support the business practice of the organization.

The mission of MMS is to acquire the right materials & services at the right time, at the right place in the right quantity of the right quality, at the right cost from the right supplier. MMS is a modular system with Graphical User Interface based on Windows standard.
Purchase Requisition
It is the primary instrument for identifying materials that must be procured from outside the company. With the supporting information of the warehouse, it authorizes the purchasing department to purchase materials in specified quantities within a specified time. Depending upon the business organization chart, it controls the activities by cost center/ department/ level.
Approval Routing
The PR routes through the electronic approval of the procurement budget for availability of budget (if any defined), authorized approval authority having allowed price limit, and the concerned warehouse(s). The multi-level approvals are subject to system setup.
Request for Quotation (RFQ)
For the materials do not fall under vendor stocking or contract, it generates & releases RFQ to the selected registered vendors. It allows the user to select multiple vendors for multiple items. It supports to capture the Quotations/ bids received against RFQ as well as without RFQ. It also generates the Comparative Statement of the Quotations containing the landing cost of each item of each vendor along with the terms and conditions of the business. Thus, it also develops the vendor material relationship.
Purchase Order (PO)
It selects the competitive Vendor(s) out of the quotations and generates the Purchase Order(s). The release of a Purchase Order automatically updates the Procurement Budget. The split of a PR into multiple RFQ, multiple Purchase Order, splitting of one PR into multiple PO to a single vendor are some of the attractive features of the system.
The system also supports the multiple amendment s of PO, Repeat Purchase Order, Reminder to the vendors, the Rate Contract Maintenance, Work Contract and payment schedules. Besides the above functionality, Petty Cash Purchasing, Emergency Purchasing, Requisition for Advance/ Prepayment etc. are some of the additional features to make the system more usable.
Receipts
MMS is well equipped with the tools to receipt the consignment coming through any mode of transport. Whatever may be the mode of transport, the final entry to the site is always either by road or railways. For Air & Ship consignment, the unloading of material at port and dispatch to unloading sites are maintained by the system.

The receipt process of Roadways consignment starts from the Gate Entry Management where date & time stamps with minimum consignment information are captured. This is followed by the creation of a Stores Inward Entry (or Pre GRN). Once a consignment is transformed into Pre-GRN, the physical & quality inspection is carried out by the warehouse in-charge or the technical person authorized by the Purchase Requester. After getting the inspection report, the warehouse in-charge may convert the receipt into Goods Receipt Note (GRN), where the discrepancies and acceptance of materials is finalized. If needed, the discrepancy notes go to the respective vendor.

For raw materials, it generates the Movement Order by the pile manager or warehouse in-charge. The objective of MO is to pre-define the unloading location, pile number and maximum quantity could be received. It takes care of user-defined tolerance.
Material Usage
To get issued any materials from a warehouse is always through a Material Issue Request. It allows the users at cost centers/ shop floors to create Material Issue Request followed by the electronic approval of the authorized approval authority. It needs to be cleared by the Consumption Budget predefined by the management for the cost center for the category of the materials. All the approved MIR becomes electronically available to the respective warehouse facilitating with the MIR information to the issue in-charge of the warehouse to issue the material physically.

MMS also handles other transactions like sales, transfer, merger etc with appropriate usage information.
Inventory Management
The inventory Management in MMS supports the Centralized as well as distributed warehousing concept. It provides on-line screens for managing various types of inventory transactions which includes the receipts, warehouse transfer, stock adjustment, book transfer (merging of materials), material returns etc. It has got a sophisticated monitoring tool for material tracking, expediting supply, stock levels and re-order points.

The inventory valuation in MMS is unique to others. It supports multiple valuation method for independent material category broadly separating the raw materials and other spare parts.
Decision Support
On-line screens and reports with dynamic sort/ filter criteria are available to fetch the information in desired format is a mouse click away for the managers. The most used such utilities include the Material tracking, Internal/External lead-time, Due to Pay, Variance analysis to budget, Inventory Control Analysis, comparative balance between MMS and AP system etc.
Material Resource Planning & Forecasting
Based on the previous year's/ Month's demand, its Reorder-point, Min-Max planning utilities optimize the inventory investment and forecast the Materials Requirement. This is a very important and handy tool for the raw material procurement.
Vendor Performance Evaluation
Compares the overall and individual scores for the vendors in different ways enabling you to judge which vendors have the best overall score? The evaluation is based on quality, delivery and price rating with user defined weight. You will be able to know who delivers the best product, with on-time delivery, for the lowest cost.
Integration with other Modules
MMS is designed quite modular to be integrated with other modules or legacy system. The design and development ensures it scalability to embrace other functionalities effectively. However, some crucial information like Cost Center, Vendor Information, payment Terms, logistic invoice, PO, GRN etc. are commonly shared by MMS and Financial Accounting. MMS easily supports & accepts the multi-dimensional (codification segments) coding structure of any standard ERP like Oracle, Baan, SAP etc.

MMS is easily integrated with other modules like Plant Maintenance, HR, Costing, PPC, Logistic etc.
User Registry & Privileges and Security
MMS provides the administrator a user friendly tool to register the users with various levels of authorization for various functions. Thus, it provides multi level privilege (along with special privilege) and ensures the security of the system.
Common Table Maintenance
Maintains meticulously designed repository of various common tables with levels of parameters, which makes the system more flexible, user friendly and user needed.
