PortMan

The Port Management System (PortMan) is aimed at providing on-line information management to meet the operational requirements of various departments in a Port Management business process. Starting from the Vessel management, it routes through the relevant activities and hands off the responsibility at the dispatch of the cargo.

PortMan is a complete system that ensures the right scheduling, accurate inventory management and correct reporting. Each function of PortMan is unique to its functionalities, but is an integral part of the Resource Planning Mechanism having a intertwine relationship amongst Billing, Administration, Purchasing, Inventory & Warehousing. Thus, the PortMan has a seamless online interface with other relevant applications.
 

It is to provide an integrated system capable of automating the process of Import/ Export business through vessels. The rule based system supported by its dynamic analytical reporting mechanism; PortMan aligns itself with the changing business scenarios.

In order to discipline the usage of common attributes, PortMan prescribes the concept of Data codification and repository system. In fact, the logically defined codes are the main stream of the system to support a consistent data flowing amongst various functionalities. The common codes which are used across the organization are captured, maintained and utilized by the system.

Portman is basically a system with fast growth & fast movement data set starting with the registration of Berth Application (BA). Once the BA is generated, it tracks the movement of individual cargo item till it is out of Port jurisdiction. Similarly it picks up the export cargo and hands off only when it is dispatched. In the process, it incorporates the intelligent Berth Scheduling, Resource Requirement, Billing, Purchase Order, Ware-housing, Inventory transactions, Maintenance & Repair, Administration etc.
